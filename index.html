Solar System Explorer

Explore our planetary neighborhood with detailed information about each celestial body:

  • Planet Name
  • Radius (km)
  • Density (g/cm³)
  • Mass (kg)
  • Gravity (km³/s²)
  • Mean Temperature (K)
  • Albedo

Interactive features include size comparison with Earth and calculating how much a person would weigh on each planet.

Explore Solar System Model

Star Database

Discover detailed information about stars and their planetary systems:

  • Planet Name
  • Host Star
  • Spectral Type
  • Orbital Period (days)
  • Radius (Earth radii)
  • Mass (Earth masses)
  • Distance to Star (light-years)
  • Equilibrium Temperature (K)
  • Star Effective Temperature (K)
  • Star Mass (Solar masses)

Interactive D3.js visualizations include temperature comparison with the Sun, hottest/coldest star identification, and size comparisons with Earth and Sun.
